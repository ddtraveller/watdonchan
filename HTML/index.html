About Us

Wat Don Chan is a well-known Buddhist temple and orphanage located in Chiang Mai, Thailand. It is famous for its abbot, who is said to be clairvoyant, attracting many visitors seeking guidance and knowledge about their lives or futures. The temple houses thirty monks, many of whom are from Burma, and it is home to around 650 children, with 450 of them attending the government school.

Wat Don Chan also has an adjoining school and a technical college where university teachers volunteer their time to teach high school graduates.

Our Programs

Education

All children attend local schools and receive additional tutoring at the orphanage. We also provide vocational training for older teenagers preparing to enter the workforce.

Cultural & Spiritual Development

Children participate in traditional Thai ceremonies, meditation practices, and Buddhist teachings to foster spiritual growth and cultural awareness.

Life Skills

Our children learn essential practical skills including cooking, cleaning, gardening, and basic financial literacy to prepare them for independent living.

การศึกษา

การพัฒนาด้านวัฒนธรรมและจิตวิญญาณ

เด็กๆ เข้าร่วมในพิธีกรรมแบบไทยดั้งเดิม การฝึกสมาธิ และคำสอนทางพระพุทธศาสนา เพื่อส่งเสริมการเติบโตทางจิตวิญญาณและความตระหนักทางวัฒนธรรม
